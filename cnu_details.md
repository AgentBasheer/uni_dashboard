# 3. Apostille & Document Authentication

From **page 12–13 of the document**. 

### For Pakistan Applicants

Steps:

1️⃣ Get documents issued by:

* Your **Board of Intermediate Education**

2️⃣ Get **Notarization**

3️⃣ Get **Apostille from Pakistan Ministry of Foreign Affairs**

Typical order:

```
Board Certificate
↓
Notary
↓
MOFA Apostille
↓
Submit to University
```

⚠️ Apostille required for:

* Graduation certificate
* Transcript

# 9. Important Edge Cases / Notes

These are **very important rules applicants often miss**.

---

## Document Timing

Financial certificate:

```
Must be issued within 30 days
```

before submission.

---

## Incomplete Documents

If missing documents:

* Application **can be rejected**
* Even if submitted online.

---

## Admission Cancellation

Admission may be cancelled if:

* Documents are fake
* Documents mismatch application
* Visa is rejected

---

## Document Language

If documents are **not in English or Korean**:

You must submit:

```
Notarized English translation
```

---

## Passport Name Matching

Your name must match exactly on:

* Passport
* IELTS
* Academic documents

---

## Visa Step After Admission

After acceptance:

```
Pay tuition
↓
Receive Admission Certificate
↓
Apply for Korean D-2 visa
↓
Travel to Korea
```
