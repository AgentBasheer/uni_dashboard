# 2. Required Documents (Specific to JBNU)
*Based on Spring 2026 requirements.*

### A. University-Specific Forms (Print from portal)
*   **Application Form (Form 1)**
*   **Affidavit of Financial Support (Form 2)**
*   **Agreement for Personal Information Collection**

### B. Global Documents (Common to all Unis)
*   **High School Diploma** (Apostilled)
*   **High School Transcript** (Apostilled)
*   **Birth Certificate / Family Registration Certificate** (FRC)
*   **Copy of Passport** (Applicant and Parents)
*   **Language Certificate** (IELTS 7.5 achieved)

### C. Financial Proof
*   **Bank Balance Certificate**: Minimum **KRW 16,000,000** (~$13,000).
*   Must be issued within **30 days** of the submission date.

# 4. Scholarships (First Semester)
*Based on IELTS 7.5 score.*

| Eligibility | Scholarship Type | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **IELTS 8.0+** | Academic Type 1 | **100% Tuition Waiver** |
| **IELTS 7.5** | **Academic Type 3** | **50% Waiver of Tuition Fee II** |

> [!NOTE]
> JBNU provides a first-year living support grant of **KRW 1,300,000** for new students.
