# 2. Required Documents (Freshman)

The required document list appears mainly on **pages 13–15** of the guideline.

### A. Core Admission Documents
1. **Online Application Form (Form 1)**
   * Name must match passport exactly.
2. **High School Graduation Certificate**
   * Must be **Apostilled** (Pakistan is an Apostille member).
3. **High School Transcript**
   * Must be **Apostilled**.
4. **Copy of Applicant's Passport**
5. **Copies of Applicant's and Parents' ID or Passports**
6. **Official Certificate of Family Relationship**
   * For Pakistan: **Family Registration Certificate (FRC)** or Birth Certificate.
   * If not in English/Korean, requires notarized translation.
7. **Financial Support Statement (Form 3)**
   * To be filled by the sponsor (e.g., parent).

### B. Language Proficiency
* **IELTS 5.5+** or **TOPIK Level 3+** is required for most departments.
* Your **IELTS 7.5** is excellent and exceeds the minimum requirement.
* If you apply for an English track, your score will qualify for higher scholarship tiers.

### C. Financial Proof (Required for Visa/Final Admission)
* **Bank Balance Certificate**: Minimum **KRW 16,000,000** (~$13,000).
* Must be issued within **30 days** of the submission date.
* If in parent's name, submit the **Financial Guarantee Form**.
